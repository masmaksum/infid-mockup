Community Engagement in Healthcare Programs
Project: Rural Health Initiative 2024Published2025-11-15
✓ What Worked

Local health workers as trusted intermediaries increased program acceptance by 45%. Community-led education sessions proved more effective than external trainers.

⚠ Challenges

Initial resistance from traditional healers required extensive dialogue and partnership building. Language barriers in remote villages needed local translators.

→ Recommendations

Early engagement with community leaders essential for program buy-in. Allocate minimum 3 months for community relationship building before program launch.

Digital Tools for Beneficiary Tracking
Project: Education Support ProgramPublished2025-10-28
✓ What Worked

Mobile app reduced data entry time by 60% and improved accuracy. Offline-first design enabled field work in areas with poor connectivity.

⚠ Challenges

Limited smartphone access among field workers required device procurement. Initial training took longer than expected (2 weeks vs planned 1 week).

→ Recommendations

Budget for devices and extended training. Consider feature phones as alternative for very remote areas.
